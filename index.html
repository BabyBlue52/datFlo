    Mission

    Flownomics is a pseudo-stock market set up around Hip-hop artists, each artist or groups is calculated into share and an offering price. Users are able to buy and sell these shares to learn to spot trends and the necessary Financial Literacy to understand those used in the real world stock markets (e.g. NASDAQ and the NYSE.) With these metrics in place, artists listed on Flownomics are definitively ranked for hierarchy placements within any given hip-hop sub-genre.

    Flownomics also provides a comprehensive back catalog of artist works linking to reviews and discovery opportunities integrating SoundCloud, YouTube Spotify and iTunes for the user to buy or stream songs...

    Blockchain

    In order to keep our volatile market safe from fraudulant activity and double selling digital assets, we moved the platform onto the Ethereum blockchain to validate that all transactions are from verified accounts or meet the consensus.

    Each artist we issue shares under effectively becomes its own "coin" and justifies the cost of all other digital assets on the platform.
